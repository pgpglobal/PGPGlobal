The Harvard Personal Genome Project is proud to announce that, after a round of emails toÂ get consent from all the original authors, our consent forms are now available as public domain. We hope they will prove useful for other projects seeking to use open consent.

The latest version of the consent document, which includes the recent change to our minimum age of consent (lowered from 21 years old to 18 years old), is available here.
